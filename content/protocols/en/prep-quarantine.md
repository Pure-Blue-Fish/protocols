## Water Parameters (week before!)

- [ ] Biofiltration: add ammonia and verify system handles it *(If insufficient - week to improve)*
- [ ] Salinity: compare to fish source, verify minimal difference
- [ ] Temperature: compare to fish source *(Max difference: 5Â°C)*
- [ ] pH: check and record

## Cleaning and Tank Preparation

- [ ] Clean fish tanks (including air and oxygen piping)
- [ ] Clean and disinfect entire quarantine area
- [ ] Replace outlet water nets with size suitable for fingerlings

## Fish Receiving Equipment

- [ ] Fish acclimation tanks
- [ ] pH meter + thermometer
- [ ] Salinity meter
- [ ] Recording slates
- [ ] Water testing pitchers
- [ ] Aeration and oxygenation piping
- [ ] Oxygen meter
- [ ] Ammonia absorbing material
- [ ] Appropriately sized hand nets
- [ ] Work buckets + scale

## Laboratory Preparation

- [ ] Ammonia measurement kits ready *(Testing will be done in quarantine, not lab)*
- [ ] Dissection tools and microscope ready *(Fish examinations only after intake complete!)*
