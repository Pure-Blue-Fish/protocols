## Important! Do Not Feed on Weighing Morning

- [ ] Weighing accuracy (no food in stomach)
- [ ] Reduced stress for fish

## Required Equipment

- [ ] Hand nets (catchers)
- [ ] Weighing bucket
- [ ] Scale (0.1 gram precision)
- [ ] Air/oxygen diffuser
- [ ] Oxygen meter
- [ ] Recording notebook
- [ ] Calculator

## Work Steps

- [ ] Fill bucket halfway with system water
- [ ] Place on scale and tare
- [ ] Catch fish with wall net or catchers
- [ ] Transfer to bucket without excess water
- [ ] Record total weight
- [ ] Transfer fish back while counting each one
- [ ] Calculate average: total weight รท fish count
- [ ] Update in computer and feeding table
